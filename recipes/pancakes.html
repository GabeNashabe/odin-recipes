Pancakes

Description

Looking for an easy and simple to make pancake recipe? This is the recipe for you. Topped with syrup, strawberries or whipped cream, these pancakes are impossible to resist.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flower
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • cooking spray
