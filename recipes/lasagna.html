Lasagna

Description

Lasagna originate din Italy during the Middle Ages and since then has been a staple meal in all Italian households. With it's easy to acquire ingredients and it's simple to follow steps, you too can enjoy this delicious tasting twist on a traditional pasta.

There are three main parts to a lasagna

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
